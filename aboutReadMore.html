More about Cookie Homes

Nestled within the exquisite confines of the apartment complex, Cookie Homes presents an extraordinary living experience with its array of impressive features.

  • Rooftop Sky Lounge: A luxurious rooftop sky lounge that offers panoramic views of the city skyline, providing residents with a stunning space to socialize, entertain guests, or simply unwind while enjoying breathtaking vistas.
  • Indoor Spa and Wellness Center: An indulgent indoor spa and wellness center equipped with state-of-the-art facilities such as steam rooms, saunas, hot tubs, and massage rooms. Residents can indulge in relaxation and rejuvenation without leaving the comfort of their home.
  • Outdoor Movie Theater: An open-air movie theater where residents can enjoy the latest blockbusters under the stars. With comfortable seating and a large screen, this feature adds a touch of entertainment and creates a unique cinematic experience.
  • Pet-Friendly Facilities: An apartment complex that welcomes pets and offers dedicated pet-friendly facilities such as a dog park, grooming station, and even a pet daycare. It ensures that residents' furry friends are also well taken care of and can enjoy the apartment community to the fullest.
  • Virtual Reality Gaming Room: A cutting-edge virtual reality gaming room equipped with the latest gaming consoles and immersive VR technology. Residents can dive into virtual worlds and enjoy thrilling gaming experiences without leaving the comfort of their home.
  • Zen Meditation Garden: A serene and tranquil Zen meditation garden where residents can escape the hustle and bustle of daily life. Complete with lush greenery, peaceful water features, and meditation spaces, this feature provides a serene sanctuary for relaxation and introspection.
